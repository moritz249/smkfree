Privacy / Legal

Private non-commercial project

Purpose

SMKFREE is a private, non-commercial smoke-free progress tracker. It creates a receipt-style summary from the details you enter. The app is for motivation only.

No medical or financial advice

Health benefits, estimated life back, cigarette counts, and cost totals are rough motivational estimates. They are not medical, financial, or professional advice. Actual effects and costs vary from person to person.

Local storage

Your entries are saved only in your browser using localStorage, so the page can restore your details when you come back. SMKFREE does not run user accounts, analytics, advertising, or tracking scripts. Use Reset inside the app to delete the locally saved app data from your browser.

Hosting

The site is hosted with GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical access data, such as IP address and browser information, to provide and secure the hosting service.
